About Us

Where Tradition
Meets Taste

We are a proud franchise of Amrittulya — a name that translates to "comparable to nectar". Our mission is simple: to serve you the most authentic, freshest chai prepared with premium ingredients, traditional recipes, and a whole lot of love.

Every cup is brewed fresh in our signature brass vessels, using the finest tea leaves, farm-fresh milk, and a secret blend of natural spices — just like the way it has always been done in Indian households.
